# Connect your phone

When `BingeWatchMe.app` starts it automatically opens a setup page in your
browser showing a QR code.

## Steps

1. Make sure your phone is on the same WiFi network as your Mac
2. Open your phone camera
3. Point it at the QR code on the setup page
4. Tap the link that appears
5. The remote UI opens in your phone browser

The URL contains your token automatically — you do not need to type anything.

## Reconnecting

The remote URL contains your token. If you bookmark it on your phone you can
open it directly next time without scanning the QR code again.

If the connection drops the phone UI will reconnect automatically.
