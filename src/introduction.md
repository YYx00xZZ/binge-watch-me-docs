# binge-watch-me

A self-hosted media remote controlled from your phone. Run it on your Mac,
scan a QR code, and control Netflix from your phone browser — no app install needed.

## How it works

1. A lightweight daemon runs in your Mac menu bar
2. A browser extension connects the daemon to Netflix
3. Your phone connects via a QR code and controls playback through a web UI
